I shot my first photo wedding in 2016, filmed my first wedding in 2018, and in 2021 Jess officially joined me behind the camera. The rest is history. Together we built Nightfox Films, a Rochester based
duo of 90s kids with a love for Super 8, camcorders, and modern 4K. I’m the techy one obsessed with editing and mixed media storytelling, while Jess brings a delicate and intuitive eye, and we
both share the same passion for capturing real moments in full no matter the camera in our hands.

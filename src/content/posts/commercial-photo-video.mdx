I’ve been behind a camera handling photography, videography, and audio for over a decade and honestly, I still get a kick out of every project, big or small. I handle everything in house, from story boarding, shooting to editing, which keeps things simple and personal. If you just need someone to capture any simple event, I’ve got you. If you want the whole nine yards with photo, video, and live streaming, that’s what I really geek out on.

I regularly team up with my partner in crime, Jessica from Nightfox Films. Together, we’ve tackled everything from social media management to podcast production, and we’re always looking for new ways to mix things up. Lately, I’ve been especially into using vintage formats, like VHS or Super 8 film. I’d love to see it as a part of modern branding. It could be a fun way to stand out!
